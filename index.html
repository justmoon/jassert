Include jQuery and jAssert

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/1.4.2/jquery.min.js"></script>
<script type="text/javascript" src="http://ajax.jassert.com/ajax/libs/jassert/1.0/jassert.min.js"></script>

Or you can just download a pre-made template.

Write your tests

jAssert.runSuite('Basic math', function () {
	//           Name of test             Expected result      Actual result
	this.runTest('Addition'             , 4                  , 2 + 2);
	this.runTest('Subtraction'          , 6                  , 10 - 4);
	this.runTest('Fail'                 , "cool"             , "not cool");
});

Enjoy the result


	
Now you have your entire test suite contained in one HTML file!
